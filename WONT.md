# WONT

Defense convention used over an opponent's 1NT opening.  Modified version of DONT

---

## The Convention

- 2♣ - clubs and another suit, with a 5-4 or better shape
  - Advancer passes if clubs is not the worst possible suit
  - If it is the worst possible suit, advancer bids 2<span class="r">♦</span> and overcaller passes or corrects
- 2<span class="r">♦</span> - Diamonds and a major (5-4 or better) 
  - Advancer passes if Diamonds is not the worst possible suit
  - If it is the worst possible suit, advancer bids 2<span class="r">♥</span> and overcaller passes or corrects
- 2<span class="r">♥</span> - 6+ hearts
- 2♠ - 6+ spades 
- Double - EITHER a 6-card minor or 5-4 majors - relay bid
  - Advancer must bid 2♣
  - With a 6 card minor, overcaller will bid it
  - With both majors, overcaller will bid 2H, and advancer will pass or correct
