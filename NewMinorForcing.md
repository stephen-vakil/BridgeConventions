# New Minor Forcing

Used by responder after a 1NT rebid by opener to ask about opener's shape, particularly major-suit length.

---

## When It Applies

**Auction:** 1m – 1M – 1NT – 2♣ or 2<span style="color:red;">♦</span> (the *other* minor = new minor)
**Auction:** 1m – 1M – 2NT – 3♣ or 3<span style="color:red;">♦</span> (the *other* minor = new minor)

- Responder bids the **unbid minor** as an artificial, forcing bid
- Does **not** show clubs/diamonds — it's asking opener to describe further

**Requirements for responder:**
- Invitational or better values (typically 10+ HCP)
- Wants more information before placing the contract
