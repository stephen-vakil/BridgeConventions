### Overcalls

**Simple (1-level):** 8–15 HCP; new suit by advancer is non-forcing constructive

**Jump overcall:** Intermediate — sound when vulnerable, light when non-vulnerable

**NT overcalls:**
- Direct 1NT: 15–18 HCP, systems on (Stayman, transfers)
- Balancing 1NT: 12–14 HCP
- Jump to 2NT: 2 lowest unbid suits ([Unusual 2NT](Unusual2NT.md))

**[Michaels Cuebid](MichaelsCuebid.md):**
- Over a minor: both majors
- Over a major: other major + a minor

### Defense vs. Opponent's 1NT
**[Landy](Landy.md):** 2♣ = 5/4 or better in both majors

### Carding
- **Primary signal:** Attitude, then suit preference
- **vs. trump suit:** Lead A from AK

### Other Conventions
- **New Minor Forcing:** After 1m – 1M – 1NT, 2♣ or 2♦ = forcing, asks about opener's shape
