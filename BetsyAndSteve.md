### Overcalls

**Simple (1-level):** 8–17 HCP; new suit by advancer is non-forcing constructive
**Simple (2-level):** 11–17 HCP; new suit by advancer is non-forcing constructive(??)

**Jump overcall:** Intermediate — sound when vulnerable, light when non-vulnerable

**NT overcalls:**
- Direct 1NT: 15–18 HCP, systems on (Stayman, transfers) — range **announced**
- Balancing 1NT: 12–14 HCP — range **announced**
- Jump to 2NT: 2 lowest unbid suits ([Unusual 2NT](Unusual2NT.md))

**[Michaels Cuebid](MichaelsCuebid.md):** **Alertable**
- Over a minor: both majors
- Over a major: other major + a minor

### Alertable and Announced Bids Summary

> **Announced** = partner describes the bid aloud.
> **Alerted** = partner says "Alert" and waits for opponent to ask.

**Announced** (partner describes aloud):

| Bid | What partner says |
|-----|-------------------|
| 1NT opening | "15 to 17" |
| 2NT opening | "20 to 21" |
| Jacoby Transfer (2<span class="r">♦</span>/2<span class="r">♥</span> over NT) | "hearts/spades" |
| Texas Transfer (4<span class="r">♦</span>/4<span class="r">♥</span> over NT) | "hearts/spades" |
| NT overcall | State the range |
