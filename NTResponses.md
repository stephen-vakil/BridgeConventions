Used to find a 4-4 major-suit fit after a NT opening.

**When to use:**

## Texas Transfers

Used to transfer directly to game in a major, usually with a long suit and enough for game but no slam interest.

**Auction:** 1NT – 4♦ → opener bids 4♥; 1NT – 4♥ → opener bids 4♠

| Responder bids | Transfers to |
|---------------|-------------|
| 4♦ | 4♥ (game in hearts) |
| 4♥ | 4♠ (game in spades) |

**When to use Texas vs. Jacoby:**
- Texas: game values, no slam interest, 6+ card major
- Jacoby: game or slam possible, or need to describe hand further
