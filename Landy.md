# Landy

Defense convention used over an opponent's 1NT opening.

---

## The Convention

**2♣** over opponent's 1NT = at least **5/4 in the majors** (hearts and spades)

- The 2♣ bid is artificial — it does **not** show clubs
- Shows at least 5 cards in one major and 4 in the other
- Typically a hand that is not strong enough to double (penalty)
