# Unusual 2NT

A jump to 2NT over an opponent's opening bid to show the **two lowest unbid suits**.

---

## The Convention

**2NT** (jump) over an opponent's opening = 5+ cards in each of the two lowest unbid suits

| Opponent opens | Your 2NT shows |
|---------------|---------------|
| 1<span class="r">♥</span> | Clubs and diamonds (the two lowest unbid suits) |
| 1♠ | Clubs and diamonds |
| 1♣ | Diamonds and hearts |
| 1<span class="r">♦</span> | Clubs and hearts |
