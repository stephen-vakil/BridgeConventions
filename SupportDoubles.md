# Support Doubles and Redoubles

Used by opener to show 3-card support for responder's major when opponents interfere.

---

## Support Double

**When it applies:** Opener has opened, responder bid a major, and an opponent overcalls (through 2♠).

**Auction example:** 1♣ – (P) – 1<span class="r">♥</span> – (1♠) – **Dbl** = opener has exactly 3 hearts

| Opener's action | Meaning |
|----------------|---------|
| Double | Exactly 3-card support for responder's major |
| Raise to 2M | 4-card support |

**Why it matters:**
Responder now knows whether to expect 3- or 4-card support when bidding game or slam, which affects how they evaluate their hand.

**After the support double, responder knows opener has exactly 3-card support. Responder's options:**

| Responder action | Meaning |
|-----------------|---------|
| 2 of own major | Minimum hand; **does NOT promise 5 cards** — may be only 4 |
| 3 of own major | Invitational (typically 8–10 pts with 5+ cards) |
| 2NT | Invitational (8–10 pts, no major interest) |
| 3NT / 4M | Game-forcing, places contract |
| New suit | Natural and forcing (game interest) |
| Pass | Convert to penalty double; Happy to defend against their overcall |

**Key principle:** Rebidding the major at the 2-level after a support double is a minimum, non-forcing action. It does **not** promise 5 cards in the major — opener has shown 3, and together you know you have a 7-card fit (possibly 8 if responder holds 5). Responder passes or raises based on combined values, not assumed trump count.

**With 3-card support from opener:**
- 7-card fit (4+3) is playable but not ideal — prefer NT at higher point counts if the hand is balanced.
- 8-card fit (5+3) plays normally — bid the major.

---

## Support Redouble

**When it applies:** Same situation as the support double, but the opponent **doubles** instead of overcalling.

**Auction example:** 1♣ – (P) – 1<span class="r">♥</span> – (Dbl) – **Rdbl** = opener has 3-card support for hearts

| Opener's action | Meaning |
|----------------|---------|
| Redouble | 3-card support for responder's major |
| Raise to 2M | 4-card support |

**Applies through:** 2♠ (same as support double)
